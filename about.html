About This Site

What Bench Press Mastery is for and how to get the most out of it.

Our Mission

The purpose of this site is to teach you how to bench press safely and efficiently. The goal is that someone who has never touched a bench press before will be able to perform a solid bench press after reading through these pages.

Everything is kept simple, practical, and backed by trusted sources so you can spend less time searching and more time pressing.

What You'll Find Here

The How To page covers the rules of a valid bench press, setting a proper arch, and why bar path matters. The Equipment page walks through the bar, plates, dumbbells, and bench you'll need.

Start at the Home page for the fundamentals of what the bench press is and why it works.

Get In Touch

Have a question about your bench press, spotted something that could be improved, or just want to talk lifting? Feedback is always welcome.

Every suggestion helps make this site a better resource for everyone chasing a stronger press.
